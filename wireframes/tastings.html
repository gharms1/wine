Signature Tour and Tasting



A 90-minute experience featuring a walk through our vineyards and cellars, concluding with a sit-down guided wine tasting.

This in-depth winery tour follows the path of the grape from the vineyard to the cellar to the finished wine. Guests walk into the On the Vine, visit the winemaking cellars (including fermentation and barrel aging) and receive a seated educational tasting of three wines with one of our wine education specialists. Advanced reservations are recommended.

DURATION: 90 Minutes

PRICE: $35/Person

HOURS: Offered year round, with increased frequency due to seasonality

NOTES: Limited to 15 guests. Advanced reservations are recommended. Due to the nature of this tour, children under 13 years of age are not permitted.
